Terms of service and privacy statement

Mandatory cookies

By creating a user, you agree to the following:

  • We will store your username in our database. We need this to log which turn you are on, which civs are in your game, and what their censuses are
  • We will store an encrypted version of your password, for authentication purposes. We use bcrypt for this.
  • We will store your game data, so we can calculate your turn order and display historical data.
  • No third party will be able to see this data.

Optional cookies

In addition to the data we need to run the site, we may use optional cookies to improve your experience. These cookies are not required for the core functionality of the app, and you can opt out of them.

  • We will use cookies to remember your UI preferences (e.g., theme, layout settings) between sessions.
  • We will collect anonymized usage data to help us understand how users interact with the app and improve its design and performance. This data is never linked to your account.
  • We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising networks.
  • You can control your cookie settings through your browser or device, and disable optional cookies at any time.
